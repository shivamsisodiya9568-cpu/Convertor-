FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our converter

Is this service completely free?

Yes! Our Image to PDF converter is 100% free to use. There are no hidden fees, no watermarks, and no registration required. Convert as many images as you need, whenever you need.

Are my images safe and private?

Absolutely! All processing happens directly in your browser. Your images are never uploaded to any server, ensuring complete privacy and security. Your data stays on your device at all times.

What image formats are supported?

We support all common image formats including JPG, JPEG, PNG, and WEBP. You can upload multiple images of different formats and combine them into a single PDF document.

Is there a file size limit?

Since all processing happens in your browser, the limit depends on your device's memory. Generally, you can upload images up to 10MB each. For best performance, we recommend keeping individual images under 5MB.

Can I rearrange the images before converting?

Yes! After uploading, your images appear in a preview grid. You can remove any image you don't want, and the remaining images will be included in the PDF in the order they appear.

Does it work on mobile devices?

Yes! Our converter is fully responsive and works perfectly on smartphones and tablets. You can convert images to PDF on the go, directly from your mobile browser without installing any apps.
